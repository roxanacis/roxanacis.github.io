Year 3 Final Project

Bewdley Rowing Museum
The aim of this project was to create a sophisticated scheme that would respond
to the identity of Bewdley and the specific site conditions.

Year 3 Final Project

Bewdley Rowing Museum represents a diagrammed journey through a space of contrasts- fixed and movable,vertical and horizontal, old and new, temporary and permanent. The initial diagrams have imposed the triangular building section, which has then been subjectively sculpted.
